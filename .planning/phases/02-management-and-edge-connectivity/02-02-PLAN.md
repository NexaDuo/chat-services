  <files>infrastructure/terraform/envs/production/main.tf, infrastructure/terraform/modules/cloudflare-dns/main.tf, infrastructure/terraform/modules/cloudflare-tunnel/main.tf</files>

    - Extract `tunnel_token` from output: `terraform output -raw tunnel_token`.

  </verify>

  <action>
